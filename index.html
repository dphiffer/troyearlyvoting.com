Early Voting Info

Registered voters can cast early ballots at any of these locations:

  1. Troy Atrium
    4 3rd St, Troy, NY 12180
  2. Town of Brunswick Office Building
    336 Town Office Rd Troy, 12180
  3. Schodack Town Hall
    265 Schuurman Rd Castleton, 12033
  • Sat Oct 23: 9 a.m.–2 p.m.
  • Sun Oct 24: 9 a.m.–2 p.m.
  • Mon Oct 25: 12 p.m.–8 p.m.
  • Tue Oct 26: 9 a.m.–5 p.m.
  • Wed Oct 27: 12 p.m.–8 p.m.
  • Thu Oct 28: 9 a.m.–5 p.m.
  • Fri Oct 29: 9 a.m.–5 p.m.
  • Sat Oct 30: 9 a.m.–2 p.m.
  • Sun Oct 31: 9 a.m.–2 p.m.

More information is available from the Rensselaer County Board of Elections and NY State Board of Elections.
